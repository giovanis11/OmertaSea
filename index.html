FAQ

Questions people ask before booking a boat charter in Halkida.

Clear answers help guests plan faster and help search engines understand exactly what Omerta Sea offers.

Where do Omerta Sea charters depart from?

Omerta Sea charters depart from Halkida, Greece, making it easy to plan a private day cruise around Evia and the nearby coast.

Are the charters private or shared with other guests?

Every booking is private. The boat, timing, and route are arranged for your own group only.

What types of boat trips can I book in Halkida?

Guests can book private day cruises, sunset trips, swimming-stop outings, and flexible custom coastal routes based on the day and the experience they want.

How do I request availability for a charter?

Send your preferred date, group size, and trip style by phone, email, WhatsApp, or the contact form and Omerta Sea can guide you from there.
