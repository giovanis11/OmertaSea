About Us

Your Gateway to
the Euboean Gulf

Based in the historic city of Halkida, we offer premium boat charter experiences along the stunning coastline of Evia and the Euboean Gulf. With years of passion for the sea, our team ensures every voyage is safe, comfortable, and truly memorable.

Whether you're seeking a relaxing day on the water, a sunset cruise, or an island-hopping adventure, our well-maintained fleet and knowledgeable crew are ready to make it happen.

Experiences

Unforgettable Moments on the Water

From serene sunset cruises to thrilling island adventures, we craft experiences tailored to you.

Sunset Cruises

Watch the golden sun dip below the horizon as you glide across the calm Euboean Gulf. An unforgettable evening with refreshments on board.

Island Hopping

Discover the hidden gems of the Sporades and Evia coastline. Visit secluded beaches, picturesque villages, and pristine swimming spots.

Day Trips

Spend a full day exploring the coastline around Halkida. Swim in turquoise waters, snorkel over reefs, and enjoy a seafood lunch by the sea.

Private Events

Celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, or corporate events aboard our premium vessels. Customized packages with catering available.
